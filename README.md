# Awesome PH EdTech [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

Curated education technology for the Philippines — K-12, college, ALS, and the messy stuff in between (spotty data, MATATAG alignment, AMIS quirks).

Not a directory of every DepEd PDF. Stuff here is either **used at scale**, **open source**, or **worth knowing** if you build for Filipino learners.

## K-12 & national platforms

- **[Khan Academy Philippines](https://khanacademyphilippines.org/)** — Free math/science on Khan's platform, localized for DepEd's MATATAG curriculum. In 2,000+ schools; Smart gives bundled data for KA access.
- **[Khanmigo](https://www.khanacademy.org/khanmigo)** — Khan's AI tutor/teacher assistant. Free nationwide through the KAPH + DepEd rollout (Dec 2024). Lesson plans, student prompts, usage tracking for teachers.
- **[DepEd Commons](https://commons.deped.gov.ph/)** — DepEd's main delivery site for SLMs, video lessons, and interactive ebooks. Zero-rated on Globe/Smart with DICT support — the default during distance learning.
- **[LRMDS](https://lrmds.deped.gov.ph/)** — Learning Resources Management and Development System. Catalogue + repository for region/division/school materials. Where teachers hunt for digitized modules and TLM metadata.
- **[DepEd OER](https://www.deped.gov.ph/alternative-learning-system/resources/oer/)** — Open Educational Resources hub under Digital Rise. Localized, remixable materials for ICT-assisted teaching — especially schools with weak internet.
- **[OER Project Sulong](https://sites.google.com/deped.gov.ph/icts-edtech/oer-project-sulong)** — ICTS-EdTech's OER curation program. Explains how OER ties into DCP schools and teacher digital literacy training.
- **[DepEd TV (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@DepEd_PH)** — Broadcast-style lessons on TV and YouTube. Still useful where bandwidth is thin and a teacher needs a ready-made explainer.

## Programming & CS education

- **[CodeChum](https://www.codechum.com/)** — Cebu-based auto-grader for intro CS courses (C, Java, Python, web, databases). CHED-aligned outlines, cheating detection, Moodle grade sync. Ubiquitous in PH college CS departments.
- **[CodeChum on GitHub](https://github.com/CodeChum)** — Official org repos (offline installers, quiz tooling, draw.io libs). Not the full platform, but useful if you integrate with their stack.

## Open source & indie tools

- **[EduVisualsPH](https://eductools.ph)** — Interactive STEM visualizers (physics, chem, bio, math) for PH classrooms. PWA, offline-capable, MDX explainers with sources. [Source](https://github.com/smmariquit/eductools).
- **[Abakada](https://github.com/Abakada-org/abakada)** — PWA directory of 1,000+ free/open tools for Filipino students and teachers. Offline, Tagalog/Cebuano/etc. UI, no backend. Good starting point when someone asks "may libre ba nito?"
- **[Linaw](https://github.com/GReturn/Linaw)** — Reading helper: explains academic English *in context* in seven PH languages (not dumb literal translation). Chrome extension + PDF viewer. Built for English-medium textbooks.
- **[Basa](https://github.com/carmelyne/basa-ai)** — Adult literacy app in Filipino. Shame-free lessons, browser TTS (`fil-PH`), AI tutor. Narrow scope, done properly.
- **[CSE Classroom](https://github.com/mrkjyqnt/cse-classroom)** — Unofficial Civil Service Exam reviewer. Flashcards, timed 80-item practice exam, unlock progression. Sourced from the public CSC reviewer — not affiliated with CSC.
- **[Landas](https://github.com/HitsukiMok/Landas)** — Gamified K-12 learning aimed at neurodivergent students (ADHD, autism, dyslexia). Quest-based structure; early but the problem statement is real (EDCOM 2 proficiency cliff).

## University & campus tools

- **[uplbtools](https://uplbtools.me/)** — Open-source student utilities for UPLB. Privacy-first, client-side, mentored contributor org. [GitHub](https://github.com/uplbtools).
- **[Room TBA](https://room-tba.uplbtools.me/)** — "Saan ang ___ sa UPLB?" Room finder with schedules, building filters, jeepney routes, offline PWA. Updated each sem. [Source](https://github.com/uplbtools/room-tba).
- **[Elbi GradeSim](https://gradesim.stimmie.dev/)** — Browser extension: reads AMIS grades, computes GWA, simulates Latin honor targets. Data stays local. [Source](https://github.com/uplbtools/gradesim).
- **[Freshie Guide](https://guide.stimmie.dev/)** — Onboarding site for incoming UPLB freshmen (enlistment, dorms, orgs, campus life). [Source](https://github.com/smmariquit/freshie-guide).
- **[UPLB CASA](https://www.uplb.casa/)** — CMSC 128 project: centralized housing portal replacing manual forms for UPLB accommodation (applications, room assignment, billing, audit logs). [Source](https://github.com/smmariquit/comsci-128).

## Offline & low-connectivity

- **[Project DANILO](https://github.com/nashczmo/Project-DANILO-Offline-AI-Powered-LMS)** — Offline LAN LMS with local AI tutor and lesson generation. Docker-deployed, DepEd-aligned. Built for schools where "cloud-first" is a joke.
- **[Moodle](https://moodle.org/)** — Boring but proven. ADB/DepEd last-mile school projects used Moodle over LAN for tablet DLMs when internet didn't exist. Still the fallback LMS when you need self-hosted and offline sync.
